INERTIA E-SPORTS (Ines) is the first team of Bangladesh to qualify PMPL SOUTH ASIA S1.It's aslo the first team which has played all the PMPL from Bangladesh . Previous name of INERTIA E-SPORTS was Instict E-SPORTS(ines).


Owner of INERTIA E-SPORTS is Tanvir Rahman(T34R).The journey of INERTIA E-SPORTS begin with PUBG MOBILE BANGLADESH CHALLENGE 2019.
